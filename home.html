About Us

GLARE PIX GRAPHICS (GPG)

Glare Pix Graphics is a team of expert graphic designers and image editors. The team is a specialist in image post-production, Such as Professional Clipping Path and Multi Path, Background Removal, Photo Retouching, Jewelry Image retouching, Ghost Mannequin, Shadow and Reflection Creation, Color Correction, Image masking, Image resizing and cropping, Image optimization for eCommerce website and other business.
